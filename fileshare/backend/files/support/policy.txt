ASTRA TECHNOLOGIES - IT POLICY UPDATE

To maximize employee efficiency and minimize time wasted on logins, we have rolled out our new Unified Authentication System (UAS). 

From now on, your primary session cookie ("token") is globally valid across ALL internal corporate services. This includes:
- The Support Admin Panel
- The Staff KPI Portal
- The Astra Corporate Messenger
- The Internal Fileshare

DO NOT share your token cookie with anyone. If your token is compromised and someone ruins your KPI, the company will not compensate you, and your pay will be docked accordingly.
